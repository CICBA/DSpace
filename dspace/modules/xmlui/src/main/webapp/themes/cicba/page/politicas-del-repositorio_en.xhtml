REPOSITORY POLICIES

CIC-Digital is governed by different policies created, among other things, for defining the type and characteristics of the content hosted in the repository, the rights inherent to its authors, the restrictions imposed to its access, those actions to be defined to preserve it and the different strategies implemented as to increase the visibility of the whole production from CICBA. These policies can be divided in many topics, among them:

  • Content policies
  • Data access policies
  • Deposit policies
  • Preservation policies
  • Visibility policies
  • Metadata policies
  • Service policies
